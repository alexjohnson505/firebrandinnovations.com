Who We Are

Firebrand Innovations develops technology and sells intellectual property. The company began as a way to monetize videoconferencing IP owned by the founder. We recently completed development of a fully deployable prototype of our first product, VideoConversation™. We are now actively pursuing buyers for this IP product. Future work will involve developing new IP in various spaces for purchase or license by interested parties.

Future Developments

VideoConversation is a flexible and affordable video conferencing technology for portraying fluid and natural team dynamics. Our technology utilizes multiple automatically controlled cameras to display your team at their best. We are now seeking partnerships with video conferencing companies interested in licensing our technology for integration with existing commercially available video conferencing systems.
